    MR signal models¶

    This section covers models that describe how the measured MR signal S depends on electromagnetic properties, such as the relaxation rates R1, R1 and R2* or the magnetic susceptibility , and on MR sequence parameters such as TR and TE.
    The exception is section “Magnitude models: DCE - R1 in the fast water exchange limit, direct relationship with indicator concentration”, in which the model describes how the MR signal depends directly on the indicator concentration.
